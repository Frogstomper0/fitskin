Skin Needling

Boost collagen, smooth texture and soften the look of scars with minimal downtime.

Book now See details

How it works

Collagen induction therapy creates precise micro-channels that trigger the skin’s natural healing response. This encourages new collagen and elastin, improving texture, fine lines and the appearance of acne scarring.

Benefits

  • Smoother texture and refined pores
  • Softens fine lines and acne scarring
  • Brightens overall tone and firmness

Good for / Not for

  • Good for: uneven texture, enlarged pores, post-acne marks, early lines
  • Not for: active infection, open lesions, recent isotretinoin, pregnancy

What to expect

  • Appointment time: 45–60 minutes
  • Sensation: tolerable; a warm/scratchy feeling
  • Downtime: 1–3 days of pinkness and mild dryness

Aftercare

  • Keep skin clean and hydrated; avoid active exfoliants for 3–5 days
  • SPF 30+ daily; avoid heat/sweat for 24 hours
  • Pair with LED within 48 hours to calm and speed recovery

This page provides general information and is not a substitute for personalised advice. We’ll assess suitability in-clinic.
