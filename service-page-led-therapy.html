LED Light Therapy

Calm redness, speed recovery and support clear, healthy skin.

Book now See details

How it works

Targeted light wavelengths (red, near-infrared and/or blue) are delivered to the skin to stimulate cellular energy, calm inflammation and support repair. This non-invasive treatment is comfortable and downtime-free.

Benefits

  • Reduces redness and calms irritation
  • Speeds post-treatment recovery
  • Helps clear breakouts and balance oil
  • Supports collagen for firmer, brighter skin

Good for / Not for

  • Good for: sensitivity, post-procedure care, breakouts, dullness
  • Not for: photosensitivity, photosensitising medication, epilepsy triggered by light

What to expect

  • Appointment time: 20–30 minutes
  • Sensation: gentle warmth; relaxing
  • Downtime: none

Aftercare

  • No special care needed; continue regular skincare
  • Use SPF 30+ daily
  • Ideal between treatments or as an add-on to facials/needling

We’ll assess suitability in-clinic and tailor wavelengths to your skin.
