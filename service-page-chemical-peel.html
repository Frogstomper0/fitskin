Chemical Peel

Brighten, smooth and clarify with a tailored clinical peel—minimal downtime, visible glow.

Book now See details

How it works

We select the right strength and acid blend (e.g. AHA/BHA) for your goals on the day, applying a controlled peel to lift dull surface cells, decongest pores and encourage a fresher, more even look. Options range from glow-giving enzyme/AHA to more corrective blends.

Benefits

  • Smoother texture and refined pores
  • Brighter, more even tone
  • Helps clear congestion and breakouts
  • Preps skin to absorb home care better

Good for / Not for

  • Good for: dullness, uneven tone, congestion, early lines
  • Not for: active cold sores, open lesions, recent isotretinoin, pregnancy (for certain acids)

What to expect

  • Appointment time: 30–45 minutes
  • Sensation: mild tingling/heat that settles quickly
  • Downtime: 0–3 days; light dryness/flaking possible depending on peel strength

Aftercare

  • SPF 30+ daily; avoid heat/exercise for 24 hours
  • No exfoliants/retinoids for 3–5 days; hydrate generously
  • Do not pick or peel; let shedding release naturally

We’ll assess suitability in‑clinic and choose the safest effective option for your skin.
