Fit Skin Facial

Our signature, tailored facial to deep cleanse, refine and hydrate—leaving skin calm, clear and glowing.

Book now See details

How it works

A results-focused facial customised to your skin on the day. After a double cleanse and gentle exfoliation (enzymatic or light AHA), we perform targeted extractions if needed, a relaxing facial massage, and a treatment mask with actives chosen for your goals. We finish with protective hydration and SPF.

Benefits

  • Deep cleanses and decongests without stripping
  • Smoother texture and refined pores
  • Reduces redness and rebalances hydration
  • Instant glow with a calm, comfortable feel

Good for / Not for

  • Good for: first-timers, dehydration, congestion, dullness, sensitivity
  • Not for: open lesions, active infection, recent strong peels/needling

What to expect

  • Appointment time: 45–60 minutes
  • Sensation: comfortable; gentle warmth during massage/mask
  • Downtime: none; mild transient pinkness possible

Aftercare

  • Hydrate and use SPF 30+ daily
  • Avoid strong exfoliants/retinoids for 24–48 hours
  • Great to pair with LED for calming and extra radiance

We’ll tailor each step to your skin and comfort on the day.
